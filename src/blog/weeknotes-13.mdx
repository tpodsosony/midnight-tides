### Music I've been listening to

- By Storm album finally dropped!! Have some mixed thoughts on it. I think conceptually its great! More unique than the groups ever been before. Particularly enjoyed the singles and GGG. I just don't think its an album with a lot of longevity in it for myself. It works for a very specific mood, but is not something I would play most of the time. Still very happy to see this duo release music and I hope for many more releases in the future :)

### Other media 🎮📚🎬

- Petal Bridge in EO2U ruled so damn hard!! Something I realized as I was mapping the floors is that they all ended up looking like flowers missing the center bud! The puzzles were mad fun too, with 19F introducing these floating platform puzzles somewhat reminiscent of the zero gravity puzzles in EO5's Untamed Garden. Right on the 20th floor in what looked like the boss room, you get sent down to fill in the center of the rest of the floors in the stratum! Felt very satisfying finally getting those centers explored, and the way the stairs were used in the puzzles was very creative! Easily the highlight of the game for me so far, and naturally Yuzo Koshiro's music here is great!

- Watched Drunken Master! Hilarious movie with absolutely bonkers stunts. Cannot imagine the physical strength and training needed for all of that. Young Jackie Chan was quite the looker I'll be honest.

- Been reading both Assassin's Apprentice and New York Trilogy! Fun starting my reread of Robin Hobb's Realm of the Elderling's, and Paul Auster is fucking with my brain in New York Trilogy still!
