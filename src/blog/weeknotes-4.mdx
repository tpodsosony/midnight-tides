### Fun things this week 

- Got a new board game! This is the first board game I've ever bought for myself actually, and it's more of a solo deck builder.
One of my greatest moments this week was setting up Unstoppable for the first time, and listening to some of my records while playing the game
for a nice screen-less few hours. 

- Researched a bunch into poetry. I've come to understand why it's always been a little hard for me to enjoy. Ingrained in me was the idea that
to enjoy poetry, I need to understand what it's about. Which is a really negative mindset, while understanding what the poem means,
as with any art there can be multiple interpretations of it. The idea of having to get the right answer before I talk about something has always been a 
major part of my anxiety, and beginning to appreciate poetry for how it makes me feel as opposed to how right I am about made so much sense to me.

- Following through with the point above, I've written down a 5 ideas that I'd be interested into making into poems! I just thought back into
moments I appreciate in my life. Some of them based on media, some based on dreams, and one based on how I felt at a certain point. I'm
excited to bring this ideas to fruition in the coming weeks!
