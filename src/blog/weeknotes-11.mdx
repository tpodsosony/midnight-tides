### Other media 🎮📚🎬

Karsa's had one of the coolest lines ever. Tehol's stupid bug puzzle destroying facists. Errant being a piece of shit. Quick Ben aura farming.

- Finally got past the third stratum in EO2U 💪. Changed my strategy a bit from hard focusing on binding and charge shennanigans to

to 'mitigate' that violence does not achieve anything other then maintain a status quo. Very good movie.
