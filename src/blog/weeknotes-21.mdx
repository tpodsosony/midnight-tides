### Other media 🎮📚🎬

- Toll the Hounds keeps on ruining me. Chapter 11 was so damn beautiful. "No, for Harllo childhood was over. Aged six."
so so devastating. And the rest of the chapter? Super funny with Leff and Scorch as usual. Insanely tense as the assassins close in on the retired Bridgeburners. So much good to talk about with this chapter!

- I have been adoring Ghost of Tsushima this past week. Normally, open world games aren't really my thing. Stuff like Horizon, or Assassin's Creed ends up boring me very quickly. Ghost of Tsushima has a somewhat similar style to those two, but alleviates my problems by way of gameplay, visuals, and story. Gameplay, while not too complex, lends well to the whole power fantasy of being a samurai. Enemies cower as your notoriety rises up, with some kills causing them to stagger in awe of Jin. The lighting is unmatched. Ghost stance switching to Kurosawa-esque aesthetics might be the coolest thing ever. And the main story / side stories all have this theme of the meaning behind having "honor" when your opponent has none and reflecting on past mistakes??? Right up my alley.
