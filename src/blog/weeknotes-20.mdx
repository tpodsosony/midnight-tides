### Other media 🎮📚🎬

- Toll the Hounds keeps getting to me. Scorch and Leff might be the dumbest characters Erikson has ever created, which says a lot. There was a scene between Berathol and Mallet that made me tear up. The two talking about ice cream, with Mallet mentioning how as a kid it used to be so much cheaper to get. Barathol imagines Mallet as a kid for half a second and smiles. The earnestness and sweetness of the moment had me cry a little :'). Karsa walking up to the insane tyrant of the constantly moving empire ran by slaves, and said tyrant instantly losing all his magic and basically killing himself was really funny to me. Tarvold Nom continues to be my favourite character ever, he's such a cunning little shit but I love him. Nimander continues to impress me.

- Started D20's Starstruck Odyssey. Been constantly cracking me up, while still having a very coherent story. Renting rooms out of a spaceship might be one of the greatest bits ever.
