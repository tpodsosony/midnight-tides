### Quote of the week


> “If we are to live ... we must take risks. Else our lives become deaths in all but name. There is no struggle too vast, no odds too overwhelming, for even should we fail - should we fall - we will know that we have lived.”

A very beautiful way of describing that need to take risks. It's something that I am aware of. There is a comfort in taking minimal risks, only taking action if you know the results, but to truly experience life one has to step out of their walls to the great unknown.
