### Other media 🎮📚🎬

- Finished the New York Trilogy! Last book was great! It wrapped up the meta fictional aspect of the trilogy, with all 3 books being written by the main character of the third book. It comes up in a way that the two previous books summarize the way the protagonist of the third feels. I do suspect based off context in the first book, that the main characters name in the third book is Paul Auster. There's a lot to think about with these books and I'll definately come back to them eventually!

- Sly Cooper 2 done! Insanely fun 3d platformer with an aesthetic that holds up fantastically after 20+ years. Had a lot of fun, but the finale was definately the weakest part. Love doing thief stuff in Canada! 

- Succession. I am obssessed with Greg and Tom toxic yaoi. 🔥🔥Can't cook a Tomlette without breaking a few Greggs🔥🔥 May possibly be the greatest show of all time.

- Watched the first episode of American Horror Story. I think the protagonists might be the dumbest people alive but it's really funny so far!

- Started Left Hand of Darkness and Toll the Hounds. LHoD has a really compelling premise, with a focus on essentially a non binary society meeting a person who is defined by their sex. Super compelling thought experiment. TtH is naturally amazing too since its more Malazan :) Enjoying both reads a lot!
