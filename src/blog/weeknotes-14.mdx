### Other media 🎮📚🎬

- Watched King of Comedy in theaters this week! I have seen it before and remembered liking it, but wow it was so much better with a crowd. I loved how people laughed at different parts, and the passion of the person who introduced the movie to the crowd! Makes me want to watch more Scorcese movies again.

- 28 Days Later was a interesting watch this week. I love what this movie is conceptually. So much respect for going in on just how dark a zombie apocalype would be and not being afraid to show it. I loved the road trip part of the movie, and Cillian Murphy going apeshit at the end. On the other hand, the editing's atrocious. Action scenes gave me headaches throughout the entire movie. There were scenes where I wasn't sure what was going on at all, even though conceptually they should have been easy to understand. I am interested in watching 28 Years Later still!
