---

title: 'Celestial longing'
pubDate: 2026-03-25
description: 'Poemtuah'
author: 'Tal'
tags: ["Celestial"]
---

Through desecrated skies I see a glimpse.  
Sparkling regardless of the lights planted.  
Around me always your alluring blinks.  
Making patterns as I am enchanted.  

Born from rocks in the void, I am a brute.  
As our compass in the dark, direct us.  
Worship the great sun, her light leaves me mute.  
And to me it was ever so precious.  

The foundation is uprooted. I burn.  
Breaths of billions heading for cessation.  
All in a desperate refusal to learn.  
My children yearn for my cremation.  

Let me turn ablaze before they do.  
And I will join the stars in the view.
