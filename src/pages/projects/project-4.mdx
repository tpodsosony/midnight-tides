This is my final DBMS2 Project for the term. I have attached my business proposal for it, which was the basis for the entire assignment. For the project, I made a database from scratch based on local Winnipeg Business's.

I chose this as I was proud of the work I had done, especially compared to where I was at the beginning of the term.

I ended up looking up a bunch of local business's for the project, ranging from all areas of the city and categorized them by what they had to offer. That took a lot of time but it helped me learn about places in the city I haven't heard of before that I'm planning on checking out in the future!

The main skills showed in this assignment was in SQL and SSMS. I was able to create views, triggers, roles, procedures, and functions for use in the database. As well I had personally uploaded all the data using SSMS's tools to transfer cvs files into data.

It took a lot of work but I'm proud of myself for having managed to do it.
